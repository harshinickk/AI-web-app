Soil quality:

This project aims to assist farmers in monitoring soil quality

Giving appropriate inputs like N,P,K values of soil helps us to predict soil quality.













Irrigation:

This project aims to assist farmers in monitoring irrigation..

Giving appropriate inputs like soil and air moisture,temperature helps us to predict dryness of soil,next water cycle and predictions to turn on or off pump.















Weed Detection:

Feeding images of plants tells you whether it is weed or healthy crop.

















Crop health:

This project aims to assist farmers in detecting plant diseases.

Feeding images of plants tells you whether plants are infected or not.
